

# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes sales performance across multiple supermarket branches to uncover trends in revenue, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, payment methods, and product performance. Using Power BI, the dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights that support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Supermarket managers need to understand which products, cities, customer segments, and payment methods contribute most to sales and profitability. Without proper analysis, identifying growth opportunities and operational improvements becomes difficult.

This dashboard answers the following business questions:

- Which city generates the highest sales?
- Which product line contributes the most profit?
- Which payment method is most preferred?
- Which customer segment spends the most?
- What are the overall sales and profit trends?

# Key Insights

### Sales Performance
- Total sales reached **116.27M** from over **1,000 customer transactions**.
- Sales remained strong throughout the period, with a slight decline in February before recovering in March.

### Product Performance
- **Food & Beverages** generated the highest profit among all product lines.
- Health & Beauty recorded comparatively lower profit, presenting an opportunity for improvement.

### Customer Analysis
- Sales were almost evenly distributed between **Member** and **Normal** customers.
- Female customers contributed slightly more to total sales than male customers.

### Payment Analysis
- **Cash** was the most preferred payment method.
- E-payment options (Epay and Card) also accounted for a significant share of transactions, showing increasing digital payment adoption.

### City Performance
- Port Harcourt recorded the highest customer satisfaction rating.
- Sales and profits were relatively balanced across the three cities, indicating consistent business performance.

---

# Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing product lines such as Food & Beverages.
- Introduce targeted promotions for lower-performing product categories.
- Strengthen customer loyalty programs to encourage more Normal customers to become Members.
- Expand digital payment incentives to improve customer convenience.
- Investigate the factors contributing to higher customer ratings in Port Harcourt and apply those best practices across other branches.
